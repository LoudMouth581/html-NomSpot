Detailed Cult Profiles

People’s Temple

Leader: Jim Jones

918 people died in 1978 in Guyana. Poisoned under "revolutionary suicide".

Aum Shinrikyo

Leader: Shoko Asahara

Sought apocalypse, released sarin gas in Tokyo. Cult blended Buddhism with sci-fi doomsday visions.

Heaven’s Gate

Leaders: Marshall Applewhite, Bonnie Nettles

Followers believed they would ascend to a spaceship behind the Hale-Bopp comet. 39 suicides occurred in 1997.

Order of the Solar Temple

Leaders: Luc Jouret & Joseph Di Mambro

French-Canadian cult with mass ritual suicides in Switzerland, Canada, and France.

Manson Family

Leader: Charles Manson

Manipulated followers to murder, hoping to incite a race war. Infamous for the murder of Sharon Tate.

Branch Davidians

Leader: David Koresh

1993 Waco siege by FBI/ATF ended in fire. 75 dead. Apocalyptic sect offshoot from Seventh-day Adventists.

Children of God

Leader: David Berg

Sexual abuse and “flirty fishing” scandals. Some celebrities were raised in the cult.

Ant Hill Kids

Leader: Roch Thériault

Canadian sadistic cult; followers were tortured and mutilated under the guise of spiritual purification.
