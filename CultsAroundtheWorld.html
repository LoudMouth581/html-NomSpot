Detailed Cult Profiles

People’s Temple

Leader: Jim Jones

918 people died in 1978 in Guyana. Poisoned under "revolutionary suicide".

Aum Shinrikyo

Leader: Shoko Asahara

Sought apocalypse, released sarin gas in Tokyo. Cult blended Buddhism with sci-fi doomsday visions.

Heaven’s Gate

Leaders: Marshall Applewhite, Bonnie Nettles

Followers believed they would ascend to a spaceship behind the Hale-Bopp comet. 39 suicides occurred in 1997.

Order of the Solar Temple

Leaders: Luc Jouret & Joseph Di Mambro

French-Canadian cult with mass ritual suicides in Switzerland, Canada, and France.

Manson Family

Leader: Charles Manson

Manipulated followers to murder, hoping to incite a race war. Infamous for the murder of Sharon Tate.

Branch Davidians

Leader: David Koresh

1993 Waco siege by FBI/ATF ended in fire. 75 dead. Apocalyptic sect offshoot from Seventh-day Adventists.

Children of God

Leader: David Berg

Sexual abuse and “flirty fishing” scandals. Some celebrities were raised in the cult.

Ant Hill Kids

Leader: Roch Thériault

Canadian sadistic cult; followers were tortured and mutilated under the guise of spiritual purification.

FLDS (Fundamentalist LDS)

Led by Warren Jeffs, this Utah-based breakaway polygamist group was prosecuted for systemic child marriages and abuse.

NXIVM

A self-help network that operated as a sex cult, branding its female members and led by convicted criminal Keith Raniere.

Unification Church ("Moonies")

Founded by Sun Myung Moon; known for mass weddings and accusations of mind control and exploitative fundraising.

Love Has Won

A bizarre spiritual cult led by “Mother God” Amy Carlson; followers claimed divine status, with reports of emotional abuse.

Church of Euthanasia

Founded by Chris Korda, this shock-value cult advocated for human extinction through radical methods like self-harm and cannibalism.

Sky Kingdom (Malaysia)

Led by Ayah Pin who professed to be all major religious figures reincarnated; the commune was demolished and outlawed.

The Family (Australia)

Anne Hamilton-Byrne’s cult kidnapped children, indoctrinated them with LSD, and claimed divine paternity.

Jackie Cult / Comrade Bala (UK)

Aravindan Balakrishnan ran a weather-forecasting cult claiming a supercomputer “Jackie” guided them; his followers were isolated until 2013.

7M TikTok Cult

A recent case where content creators were manipulated, financially exploited, and emotionally coerced by a supposed management agency.
