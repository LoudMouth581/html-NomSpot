⚡ Food Pyramid of Health ⚡

Base of the Pyramid

🍎 Fruits & Vegetables | 🥖 Whole Grains

  • At least five servings a day
  • Variety is key: include colorful veggies (leafy greens, carrots, peppers, etc.) and fruits (berries, apples, citrus, etc.).

Whole Grains

  • Brown rice, whole wheat bread, oats, quinoa, barley.
  • Rich in fiber and nutrients
  • Choose whole grains over refined (white) grains.

Middle of the Pyramid

🍗 Healthy Proteins | 🥑 Healthy Fats

  • Fish, poultry, beans, lentils, tofu, eggs, nuts, and seeds.
  • Limit red meat and avoid processed meats.

Healthy Fats

  • Olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds.
  • Avoid trans fats and limit saturated fats.

Top of the Pyramid

🍬 Sweets | 🧂 Salt & Processed Foods

  • Candy, soda, pastries, desserts.
  • Try to limit sugars.

Salt & Processed Foods

  • Chips, fast food, canned goods with lots of sodium.
  • Excess salt can lead to high blood pressure.

Alongside the Pyramid

💧 Water | 🏃 Exercise | 🍽️ Portion Control

  • Water: Stay hydrated; water is the best choice.
  • Exercise: Physical activity is essential for maintaining health.
  • Portion Control: Eat mindfully and don't overeat.
